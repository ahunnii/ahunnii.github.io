UnitedShare

UnitedShare was a submission and winner of Best Hack for Social Good at SpartaHack 2017. The premise of this app was to promote charitable acts for the homeless. Say you want to help this homeless man/ woman. However, you have nothing to give. With this app, you can post a shoutout on a live feed of others in need of help. Using Google Maps API, it takes a snapshot of the area, and allows for user input. It asks for item/s requested, the general location description, a description of the person(not a picture to protect anonymity) and posts to the feed. That way, people who are in the area and are willing to help can help.
